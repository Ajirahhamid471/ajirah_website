Table 5: ENCANTO
Trailer Movie 
Thomas loses his memory and finds himself trapped in a massive maze called the Glade. He and his friends
            try to escape from the maze and eventually learn that they are subjects of an experiment.
 
The central moral of "Encanto" is that a person's worth isn't 
                defined by their special abilities or "perfect" qualities, but by their genuine self and the love they share with 
                their family; it emphasizes the importance of embracing imperfections, supporting one another, and recognizing that
                the true strength lies in the family unit, even when facing challenges and losing magical powers.

Table 6: THE MAZE RUNNER
Trailer Movie 
An extraordinary family, the Madrigals, live hidden in 
                the mountains of Colombia, in a magical house called an Encanto. The magic of the Encanto 
                has blessed every child in the family with a unique gift - every child except one, Mirabel.
 
Never give up:
                Despite being trapped in the maze with no memories, the characters, particularly Thomas, demonstrate unwavering
                determination to escape,highlighting the importance of perseverance.
